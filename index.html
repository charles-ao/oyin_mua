About CEO

Abidemi is the head Make-Up Artist and CEO of OYINKAN Make-Up Artistry. A Graduate of The University of Ilorin from the Department of Social Works. The huge lover of eating and making food discovered her passion for Make-Up early in her University days.

Her enthusiasm for beautifying faces and boosting the self-esteem of her fellow women had comfortably pioneered her profession as a Make-up Artist. Abidemi is vastly knowledgeable about Make-Up techniques and its products. She is very recognised for her creativity, Originality and Confidence in her abilities.
